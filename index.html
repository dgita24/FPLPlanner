📘 FPL Team Planner - User Guide

🎯 Getting Started

The FPL Team Planner helps you plan your Fantasy Premier League team transfers across multiple gameweeks. You can import your current team, plan changes, and see how your budget evolves.

💰 How Banking Works

  • Bank: Shows your current available funds (starts at 100.0m for a new team)
  • Transfers: When you remove a player, their selling price goes to your bank
  • Adding Players: When you add a player, their purchase price is deducted from your bank
  • Price Modes: Use the "Show" dropdown to toggle between Selling, Purchase, and Current prices

🔧 Key Functions

  • Import from FPL: Enter your FPL Team ID in the Menu to load your current team
  • Add to Squad: Click on a player in the Transfers table, then click "Add to squad"
  • Remove Player: Click the red ✕ button on any player card
  • Swap Players: Click the blue ⇄ button to swap a player with the bench
  • Gameweeks: Use ← GW and GW → buttons to plan across multiple weeks
  • Reset: Use Reset to return to your imported team
  • Local Save/Load: Save your planning session to browser storage

📱 Mobile Usage

  • Landscape Mode: For the best mobile experience, use landscape (horizontal) orientation
  • Formation: The pitch maintains proper formation structure (1-3-5-2) on all devices
  • Scrolling: The transfers table scrolls horizontally on mobile - swipe left/right to see all columns
  • Touch Targets: All buttons are optimized for touch on mobile devices

💡 Tips

  • Plan your transfers across multiple gameweeks to maximize value
  • Consider fixture difficulty when selecting players
  • Use filters (Position, Team, Max Price) to find the right players quickly
  • Sort columns by clicking on column headers (Price, Points, Stats)
  • Check player status indicators (🚑 injured, ⚠️ doubt, ⛔ suspended)
