📘 FPL Team Planner - User Guide

Import Team via the Menu/Login Button

Enter your team ID—found by navigating to the points tab of the official FPL site. Your URL will look like: https://fantasy.premierleague.com/entry/[your id]/event/01 (where 01 at the end is GW1). Paste your ID into the 'Import' box and press Import from FPL.

Key Features

  • Player Filtering: Filter players by DEFCON points earned season to date (unlike the total CBITr figure available on the official FPL site).
  • Draft Teams: Save up to 5 draft teams — useful in preseason, or during Wildcard/Free Hit weeks.
  • Sync: Sync saved drafts across multiple devices.

Local Save and Local Load

Save and restore your planning session directly in your browser — no account or login required. Data is stored locally using localStorage (no external server or account needed). Each local save overrides the previous one. Useful for saving progress before page refresh or closing your browser.

Limitations:

  • Local saves are specific to your browser and device.
  • Your saved data will persist after closing or restarting your browser.
  • However if you clear your browser's cache, cookies, or site data (via "Clear browsing data" or similar), your saved session will be permanently deleted.
  • Local saves cannot be accessed on other browsers or devices.

Cloud Save + Cloud Load

Save drafts to the cloud (Supabase backend) via Menu/Login.

  • Name your draft file and create a password to save.
  • To reload, enter these same details in the Cloud Load boxes.
  • Drafts can be synced across devices using this feature.
  • Note: Cloud saving may become a donator-only feature if hosting/database costs require it.

Bank Balance Discrepancies

Your "bank" figure may differ from the official game because your imported team is a snapshot as of the last deadline. If a player's price changes after that point, your calculated bank on this planner might be inaccurate.

If your balance is off (e.g., lower by £0.1m due to price rises), you can manually enter the correct value in the provided field. The planner will use your edited value instead of the automatic calculation.
