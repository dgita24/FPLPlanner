Flag Badge Meanings

Red Flags (0)

  • Suspended players: Shows "0" = 0% chance of playing
  • Injured players: Shows "0" = 0% chance of playing
  • Unavailable players: Shows "0" = 0% chance of playing

Yellow Flags (25, 50, 75)

  • 25: Chance of playing ≤ 25%
  • 50: Chance of playing 26-50%
  • 75: Chance of playing 51-75%
  • Note: 100% = no flag shown

✅ Benefits of Circular Badge Design

  • Visual consistency: Matches captain/vice-captain badge style
  • More informative: Shows exact chance of playing for doubtful players
  • Cleaner appearance: No more waving canvas flags
  • Better UX: Circular badges are easier to spot and understand
  • Same size: 24px diameter like captain badges
